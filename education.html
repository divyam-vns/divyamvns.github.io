What is an IEP?

An Individualized Education Program (IEP) is a personalized plan developed for students with special needs to ensure they receive tailored educational support.

Why IEP is Important

IEPs help set measurable goals, provide necessary accommodations, and ensure students with autism receive the resources they need to succeed academically and socially.
