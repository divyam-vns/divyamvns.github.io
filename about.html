About Autism

What is Autism?

Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects communication, social interactions, and behavior. It is a lifelong condition that manifests differently in every individual.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Difficulty with communication
  • Challenges in social interaction
  • Repetitive behaviors or routines
  • Sensory sensitivities

Diagnosis and Early Intervention

Early identification of autism is key. Interventions and therapies can significantly improve social, educational, and daily life skills.
