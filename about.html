About Autism

What is Autism?

Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition affecting communication, social interaction, and behavior. It manifests differently in each individual.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Difficulty communicating
  • Challenges in social interaction
  • Repetitive behaviors or routines
  • Sensory sensitivities

Diagnosis and Early Intervention

Early detection improves social, educational, and daily life outcomes. Interventions and therapies can significantly help children with autism.
